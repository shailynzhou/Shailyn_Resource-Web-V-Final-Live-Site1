ABOUT

This website will help you find some of the best cafes to study or work at in Toronto. On each cafe page you will find information about the cafe such as their food/menu, drinks, atmosphere, quietness level, music, seating options and study aspects such as wifi and availability of power outlets. Additional helpful information will be provided such as location and hours of operation. This guide is for students and cafe lovers to help you find the best places in Toronto to study, get work done and have fun.
